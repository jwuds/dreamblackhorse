# Dream Black Horse — Offering & Differentiators

What we sell and why buyers choose us. Reference this when writing any content so claims stay accurate and on-message. (Replaces the generic SaaS "features" template — here, the "product" is horses + the buying experience.)

## What we offer
- **Friesian & black horses for sale** — quality individuals across ages and training levels, browseable at `/horses` (live inventory via the store).
- **Breeding program** — purpose-bred Friesians with attention to conformation, gait, and temperament.
- **Worldwide delivery / import support** — domestic and international shipping, with guidance on the process and costs.
- **Buyer support** — help matching horse to rider/discipline (dressage, driving, pleasure), and post-sale questions.

## Who buys from us
1. **First-time Friesian/horse buyers** — researching breed, price, suitability, and how online horse buying works.
2. **Experienced equestrians** — dressage/driving enthusiasts seeking quality bloodlines and temperament.
3. **International buyers** — need a trustworthy seller who handles export/import logistics.

## Differentiators (use these as proof points, keep them truthful)
- Specialists in **Friesians and black horses**, not a general livestock seller.
- **Transparent process** — realistic on price ranges, vetting, and shipping; no pressure tactics.
- **Worldwide delivery** experience.
- Based in **Mt Dora, Florida**; reachable at **contact@dreamblackhorse.com**.

## What we do NOT claim
- No guarantees of competition results, health outcomes, or temperament certainty beyond what records support.
- No specific prices in evergreen content (prices live on the listing pages and change).
